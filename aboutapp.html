About Our App

PetLife is an all-in-one mobile app designed for pet owners to simplify pet care.

It provides tools for managing medical records, booking appointments, finding pet-friendly services, and connecting with a community of pet lovers.

With AI features like diet planning and a veterinary chatbot, PetLife ensures pets receive the best care possible.
